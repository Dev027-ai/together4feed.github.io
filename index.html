Together4Feed is a zero-cost, community-powered network that connects food donors with NGOs to rescue edible surplus food and deliver it to hungry people across Lucknow.

Smart Matching Algorithm

Our proprietary algorithm matches food donations with NGOs based on multiple factors to ensure efficient distribution and maximum impact.

  • Location-based matching for shortest routes
  • Capacity matching based on NGO capabilities
  • Food type preferences and requirements
  • Historical performance and reliability data

AI-powered matching system

Blockchain Verification

We use blockchain technology to create an immutable record of all food donations and distributions, providing complete transparency to donors.

  • Tamper-proof records of all transactions
  • Verifiable impact reporting
  • Automated CSR compliance documentation
  • Transparent supply chain from donor to recipient
